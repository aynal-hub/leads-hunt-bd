About Us

Your Strategic Partner in Growth and Lead Generation

Fueling Your Business with High-Quality, Verified Leads

At Leads Hunt BD, we specialize in accelerating your growth through a suite of comprehensive B2B services. From precision lead generation and data management to cutting-edge digital marketing and web development, we are your dedicated partner in building a powerful sales pipeline.

Founded in 2022 by industry veterans Aynal Hossain & Sabbir Hosain, our agency was born from a vision to redefine B2B marketing. Leveraging years of frontline experience, we built Leads Hunt BD to offer a more agile, client-focused, and results-driven approach that stands out in a competitive landscape.

Why Partner With Us?

  • Unmatched Quality & Scale: We deliver a high volume of meticulously verified leads—up to 2,000 per day—ensuring your sales team connects only with genuine prospects.
  • Data-Driven Strategy & Technology: We leverage a powerful suite of industry-leading tools like LinkedIn Sales Navigator, ZoomInfo, Apollo.io, and more, to source and validate data with unparalleled accuracy.
  • End-to-End Digital Solutions: Beyond lead generation, we offer comprehensive SEO services and web development to enhance your online presence and drive organic growth.
  • 100% Valid Lead Guarantee: Our multi-step validation process, using tools like ZeroBounce and NeverBounce, ensures that every lead we provide is active and valuable.
